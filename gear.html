Gear List

A list of all of Ethan's photography gear, he uses on set during his photoshoots. From his camera body to external hard drives, the following products help increase the quality and efficiency of his photoshoots.

Nikon 3400

Quality first camera for beginners - looking into buying a full frame.

128gb SD Card

Use this and you wont have to worry about running out of storage during a shoot.

Studio Lighting

Affordable studio lighting you can set up in your living room or bedroom.

Camera Backpack

Store all of your camera gear in one place - also great for travel.

Bose Mini Speakers

Pro Tip: Always play music during a shoot - makes everyone more comfortable.

Nikon 18-300mm

Versatile lense thats works great for any type of shoot.

LaCie 1TB Hard Drive

Great to store and work with large RAW files.

Multi Disc Reflectors

Ideal for fill light when working in the studio inside.

Promaster Sling Bag

On the go bag when you only need one lense and want to keep it light.

Nikon 50mm

Perfect for portraits and provides fantastic depth.

Nikon Speedlight Flash

When in doubt bounce! Works amazing for low light shoots.

Used for all of my editing on Lightroom and Photoshop
