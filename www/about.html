About

Ethan Eisenhard is a recent graduate of a Bachelor of Science Degree in Computer Design and Development at The Pennsylvania State University. His interest in web development began at his first internship creating websites for local businesses. He learned to work with clients, design responsive websites, and optimize search engine results. Inspired by this Eisenhard went on to learn web development on a corporate level interning at Toll Brothers. On the Penn State campus, Eisenhard learned about leadership and management roles as the President and Founder of the Photography Club and the Resident Assistant of over fifty students. Using these skills, he has developed his own business designing websites and taking photos for graduating seniors.

Ethan currently works for Toll Brothers as a Web Developer and is available as a freelance photographer in the Pennsylvanna and New York state area.
